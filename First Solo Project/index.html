Visit The Woodlands in Texas

Enjoy shopping, dining, outdoor activities in this lush vibrant enviroment close to Houston.

Top three activities to do in The Woodlands

Enjoy miles of waterways to row along

The Woodlands Waterway is a great place to explore all sorts of outdoor activities: Bike riding, walking/running, rowing, taking a tour in a vintage trolley, and so much more.

Enjoy fine dining throughout the Woodlands area

World class dining with so many varities of cuisine to choose from.

Have fun shopping at many different stores

Do you love to shop? Well the Woodlands has you covered with a mall and a market district that have the latest designer labels.
